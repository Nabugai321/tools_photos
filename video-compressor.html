Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our video compression tool

How does video compression work?

Video compression reduces file size by removing redundant or less important data from the video stream. Our tool uses advanced algorithms to maintain the best possible quality while significantly reducing file size through techniques like bitrate reduction, resolution scaling, and efficient encoding.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the current maximum file size is 500MB for our free online tool. This ensures fast processing for all users. For larger files, consider compressing in smaller segments or check back soon for our premium version with higher limits.

Are my videos stored on your servers?

No, your videos are processed in your browser and never uploaded to our servers. This ensures complete privacy and security. The compression happens locally on your device, and files are automatically cleared from memory after processing.

What video formats are supported?

We support all major formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WMV, FLV, and more. The output can be saved as MP4 (recommended for compatibility), WebM (for web use), or MOV (for Apple devices).

How much can I reduce my video size?

Compression ratios depend on the original file and settings. Typically you can expect 50-80% reduction with minimal quality loss when using medium compression. Higher compression levels can achieve up to 90% reduction but may affect quality more noticeably.

Is this tool completely free?

Yes, our basic video compression tool is completely free to use with no watermarks or hidden fees. We may offer premium features in the future, but core compression functionality will always remain free.
